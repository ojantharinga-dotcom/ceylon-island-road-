Welcome to the planner for your incredible Anuradhapura ‚Üí Polonnaruwa ‚Üí Wilpattu route. Below you will find the **must-visit attractions** and the **estimated travel time** between them, ensuring a rich historical and natural experience!

Phase 1: Anuradhapura Exploration

Major Travel Time to Next City: **Anuradhapura to Polonnaruwa** ‚Üí 2 - 2.5 Hours (approx. 120 km)

Local Attractions (Ancient City Area)

Ruwanwelisaya Stupa

One of the most sacred stupas in Sri Lanka, known for its massive white dome.

Visit Time: 1 - 1.5 Hrs | Travel Time (within city): 5 - 10 Mins

Sri Maha Bodhi

The sacred fig tree, grown from a cutting of the original Bodhi Tree.

Visit Time: 1 Hr | Travel Time (within city): 5 - 10 Mins

Isurumuniya Temple

A beautiful rock temple famous for its 'The Lovers' carving.

Visit Time: 45 Mins | Travel Time (within city): 15 - 20 Mins

Phase 2: Polonnaruwa Exploration

Major Travel Time to Next Destination: **Polonnaruwa to Wilpattu Gate** ‚Üí 3 - 3.5 Hours (approx. 150 km)

Local Attractions (Medieval City Area)

Gal Vihara

Four beautiful statues of the Buddha carved into a large granite rock face.

Visit Time: 45 Mins - 1 Hr | Travel Time (within city): 5 Mins

The Quadrangle

Compact area with concentrated, well-preserved ruins (Vatadage).

Visit Time: 1.5 - 2 Hrs | Travel Time (within city): N/A

Parakrama Samudra

Huge ancient reservoir dominating the landscape.

Visit Time: 30 Mins (Viewpoint) | Travel Time (within city): 10 - 15 Mins

Phase 3: Wilpattu Wildlife

Final Stop! Enjoy the largest national park, famous for leopards and 'villus'.

Key Activities (Wilpattu Area)

Wilpattu Safari (Morning or Evening)

Jeep safari required to spot wildlife (Leopards, Sloth Bears, Elephants).

Safari Duration: 3 - 6 Hours | Travel Time (to Gate): Varies

Park Entrance / Buffer Zone

The area surrounding the park is excellent for birdwatching.

Visit Time: 1 Hr | Travel Time (from Polonnaruwa): 3 - 3.5 Hrs
