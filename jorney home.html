Explore The Island of Wonder

Handpicked destinations across Sri Lanka's beautiful landscapes.

Nuwara eliya
Take in the beautiful tea plantations, visit some stunning waterfalls, and soak up the cool, refreshing air in Nuwara Eliya.
Jaffna
Discover Jaffna Fort, visit Nallur Kandaswamy Temple, and indulge in the distinctive regional cuisine.
Ella
Take in breathtaking panoramic views, visit Nine Arch Bridge, and hike Little Adam's Peak.
Balangoda
Discover the surrounding mountain landscapes and historic caves, including the residence of Balangoda Man.
Kandy
Take in Kandy Lake, the Royal Botanical Gardens, and the hallowed Temple of the Tooth Relic.
Kataragama
In the middle of the Yala jungle, visit the holy Kataragama Devalaya, a place of pilgrimage for many different religions.
Galle
Explore the Dutch colonial architecture, see the lighthouse, and stroll around the historic Galle Fort.
Anuradhapura
Discover historic stupas, the revered Bodhi Tree, and the enormous first kingdom ruins.
