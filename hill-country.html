Hill Country Adventure

Plan your spectacular Balangoda → Kandy → Ella route with your tricycle team!

Welcome to the planner for your spectacular Balangoda → Kandy → Ella route. Below you will find the must-visit attractions and the estimated travel time between them, ensuring you maximize every minute of your trip with your tricycle team!

Phase 1: Balangoda Exploration

Major Travel Time to Next City: Balangoda to Kandy → 2.5 - 3.5 Hours (approx. 130 km)

Local Attractions (Balangoda Area)

Kuragala Raja Maha Vihara

Ancient Buddhist site with historical significance.

Visit Time: 1 - 1.5 Hrs | Travel Time (from town): 45 - 60 Mins

Belilena Cave

Site of the famous 'Balangoda Man' prehistoric remains.

Visit Time: 1 Hr | Travel Time (from town): 40 - 50 Mins

Bambarakanda Falls

Sri Lanka's highest waterfall, easily accessible for a viewpoint stop.

Visit Time: 1 Hr | Travel Time (from town): 1.5 Hrs

Phase 2: Kandy - The Cultural Heart

Major Travel Time to Next City: Kandy to Ella → 4.5 - 5.5 Hours (approx. 160 km)

Key Attractions (Kandy Area)

Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic (Sri Dalada Maligawa)

The most sacred Buddhist temple in the country, next to Kandy Lake.

Visit Time: 1.5 - 2 Hrs | Travel Time (from center): N/A

Royal Botanical Gardens, Peradeniya

Vast gardens renowned for orchids and lush collections.

Visit Time: 2 - 3 Hrs | Travel Time (from center): 20 - 30 Mins

Bahirawakanda Vihara Buddha Statue

Hilltop statue offering panoramic views of Kandy city.

Visit Time: 1 Hr | Travel Time (from center): 15 - 20 Mins

Phase 3: Ella - Scenic Hill Station

Major Travel Time: You have reached your final planned destination! Explore these spectacular sites.

Famous Sites (Ella Area)

Nine Arch Bridge

Iconic viaduct bridge set amidst lush tea plantations.

Visit Time: 1 - 1.5 Hrs | Travel Time (from town): 20 - 30 Mins (walk included)

Little Adam's Peak

Relatively easy hike offering stunning views of Ella Gap.

Visit Time: 2.5 - 3 Hrs (Hike) | Travel Time (from town): 10 - 15 Mins

Ella Rock

Challenging 4-5 hour hike with rewarding, sweeping vistas.

Visit Time: 4 - 5 Hrs (Hike) | Travel Time (from town): 15 - 20 Mins
