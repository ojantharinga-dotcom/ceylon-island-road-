Kandy Hill Country Journey

Plan your spectacular ride through the Kandy region! Find must-visit attractions and estimated travel times to make your trip perfect.

Stop 1: Kandy City Center

Sri Dalada Maligawa (Temple of the Tooth)

The most sacred Buddhist site in Sri Lanka, housing the relic of the Buddha's tooth. Ideal for cultural exploration.

Visit Time: 2 - 3 hours

Kandy Lake (Bogambara Lake)

A beautiful, artificial lake offering a peaceful walking path around its perimeter.

Visit Time: 1 - 2 hours

Kandy View Point (Arthur's Seat)

Offers a stunning panoramic view of the Temple, Lake, and the city nestled in the hills.

Visit Time: 30 mins - 1 hour

Next Travel: Kandy City Center to Royal Botanical Gardens (Approx. 20 - 30 minutes)

Stop 2: Peradeniya & Outer City

Royal Botanical Gardens, Peradeniya

One of Asia's finest botanical gardens, famous for its magnificent orchid collection and extensive greenery.

Visit Time: **2 - 4 hours**

Bahirawakanda Vihara (Giant Buddha)

A towering white statue on a hill, providing stunning city views and a tranquil, spiritual atmosphere.

Visit Time: **1 - 1.5 hours**

Udawatta Kele Sanctuary

A historical forest reserve right next to the city center, perfect for birdwatching and a jungle walk.

Visit Time: **2 - 3 hours**

Next Travel: **Royal Botanical Gardens to Three Temple Loop** (Approx. 30 - 40 minutes)

Gadaladeniya Temple

Ancient Buddhist temple built entirely on a rock, featuring unique Dravidian influences.

Visit Time: 1 - 1.5 hours

Lankathilaka Temple

A beautiful rock-cut temple known for its elaborate paintings and spectacular hillside setting.

Visit Time: 1 - 1.5 hours

Embekka Devalaya

Famous for its exceptional medieval wood carvings, especially on its pillar columns.

Visit Time: 1 - 1.5 hours

Next Travel: Three Temple Loop to Ambuluwawa (Optional Day Trip) (Approx. 1 - 1.5 hours)

Stop 4: Ambuluwawa (Day Trip)

Ambuluwawa Biodiversity Complex

Distinctive multi-religious tower on a mountain peak, offering 360-degree panoramic views of the central hills.

Visit Time: 2 - 3 hours
