JAFINA TOUR - 01 DAY

Experience the Spiritual and Cultural Heart of Northern Sri Lanka.

EXPLORE ITINERARY











Northern Heritage Highlights

Nallur Kandaswamy Kovil

One of the most important Hindu temples in Sri Lanka, dedicated to Lord Murugan. The spiritual and cultural heart of the Jaffna Tamil community.

Kilinochchiya

A town historically significant as a major location during the final stages of the civil war. Offers insight into the region's recovery.

⏱️ Our One-Day Journey Details

Morning: Spiritual & Cultural Start

7:00 AM - 8:30 AM Nallur Kandaswamy Kovil & Jaffna Public Library

Witness the morning pooja at the spiritual heart of Jaffna. Visit the iconic white library, a symbol of the city's resilience.

9:00 AM Old Park/Jaffna Fort Area

A short visit to take in the historical atmosphere and coastal views.

10:00 AM Jaffna Market

See local produce and the famous **Jaffna mangoes**.

11:30 AM Malayan Café or Local Restaurant

Stop for an authentic, spicy Jaffna vegetarian lunch served on a banana leaf—a crucial cultural experience.

Afternoon: The Island Pilgrimage (Approx. 1:00 PM - 5:30 PM)

1:00 PM Travel to KKD Jetty

Drive from Jaffna City towards the Kurikadduwan Jetty (about a 1-hour drive).

2:00 PM Boat Ride to Nainativu

Board the public ferry for the scenic ride to the island—your boat ride experience!

2:30 PM - 4:00 PM Nainativu/Nagadeepa

Visit the adjacent religious sites: Nagadeepa Purana Viharaya (Buddhist temple) and Nagapooshani Amman Kovil (Hindu temple). A powerful demonstration of **multi-faith harmony**.

4:30 PM Return Boat Ride & Travel

Take the ferry back to KKD Jetty and begin the return journey to Jaffna City.
