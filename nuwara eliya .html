The Nuwara Eliya Loop: Plains, Plants, and Ponds

Explore the "Little England" of Sri Lanka! This itinerary covers the essential hill country loop: Horton Plains, Hakgala Gardens, and Gregory Lake.

Stop 1: Horton Plains National Park (Morning Start)

World's End & Baker's Falls Hike

A protected area known for its montane grassland and cloud forest. Features the famous 9.5 km loop to the dramatic cliffs of **World's End** and the beautiful **Baker's Falls**.

Visit Time: 3 - 4 hours (must start early)

Next Travel: Horton Plains to Hakgala Botanical Gardens (Approx. 40 - 60 minutes)

Stop 2: Hakgala Botanical Gardens

Subtropical Plant Paradise

The second-largest botanical garden in Sri Lanka, renowned for its diverse collection of high-altitude flora, including roses, ferns, and temperate-climate orchids. Steeped in Ramayana legend.

Visit Time: 1.5 - 2.5 hours

Next Travel: Hakgala Gardens to Gregory Lake (Approx. 10 - 15 minutes)

Stop 3: Gregory Lake (The Heart of Nuwara Eliya)

Recreational Activities

A central reservoir offering relaxing activities like boating, speed-boating, pony rides, and picnicking along the well-maintained shores.

Activity Time: 1 - 2 hours

Nuwara Eliya Town Exploration

Located near the main town attractions, ideal for visiting the Nuwara Eliya Post Office and exploring Victoria Park afterward.

Exploration Time: 1 - 1.5 hours

Return Travel: Gregory Lake to Horton Plains area (Approx. 40 - 60 minutes)
